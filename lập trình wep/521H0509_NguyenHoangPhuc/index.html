Cat Sleeping

Here is a picture of a cat sleeping. Cats are known for sleeping a lot - in fact, they can sleep for up to 16 hours a day! When cats sleep, they often curl up into a ball and tuck their paws under their bodies to stay warm and feel safe. They may also twitch or move their paws and whiskers while they sleep, which may be a sign that they are dreaming.
